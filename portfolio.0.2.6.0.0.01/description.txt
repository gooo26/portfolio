Cinematic Production Studio Landing Page Template

A high-impact cinematic studio landing page template for showcasing film portfolios, production services, and driving project inquiries.

Kabovelo Film Farm is a cinematic production landing page template built for studios, filmmakers, and video agencies to showcase high-end visual work.

What's Included
Hero with bold headline, reel CTA, and marquee
Vision and stats section for positioning and credibility
Grid-based portfolio gallery for selected works
Detailed services "bento" grid (pre, production, post, sound, color, motion)
Behind-the-scenes masonry gallery
Conversion-focused contact form and footer
Key Features
Dark, cinematic aesthetic with large type
Portfolio-ready project cards with hover reveals
Service-focused layout tailored to production pipelines
Contact and inquiry form optimized for new projects
Smooth scroll and subtle animation effects
Ideal for launching a film or video production brand quickly, this template helps you present your reel, capabilities, and contact details in a polished, story-driven experience.
