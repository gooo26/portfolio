Real Estate Investment Portfolio Website Template

A premium dark-mode website template for real estate investment and holdings firms to showcase portfolios, interiors, ventures, and performance metrics.

Ramond Holdings is a high-end real estate investment website template designed for firms that blend architecture, finance, and global portfolios.

Features
Hero and marquee highlighting brand story, global cities, and legacy positioning
Sticky portfolio layout for residential, commercial, and industrial flagship projects
Horizontal interiors gallery to showcase premium interior design and amenities
Ventures section with scrollable cards for funds, REITs, and strategic initiatives
Stats and bento grid for AUM, years active, board highlight, and thought leadership
Elegant footer with enquiry CTA, sitemap, social links, and legal
Use cases
Real estate investment and development firms presenting a global portfolio
Family offices and property funds needing a sophisticated digital presence
Architecture-led investment brands showcasing projects and ventures to investors.
