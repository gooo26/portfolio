Video Editing & 3D Studio Landing Page Template


A dark, futuristic landing page template for a premier video editing and 3D rendering studio, perfect for showcasing portfolios, services, team, and client testimonials.

Streax Studios Landing Page Template is a dark, futuristic one-page site for video editing and 3D rendering studios.

Highlights
Hero "Nexus" section with glitch headline, system status, and bold CTA
Core protocols/USPs using animated vertical accordion panels
Manifest/about area with journey story and performance stats
Archive portfolio with horizontal scroll and 3D flip case-study cards
Evidence/testimonials in floating glassmorphism panels
Team & contact/CTA block with avatars, email, phone, and neon primary button
Use Cases
Branded homepage for video, CGI, or motion studios
Web3-focused creative or production agency site
High-impact portfolio microsite for freelancers or small teams
Built with Tailwind CSS and immersive animations, this template helps studios present capabilities, work, and trust signals in a cinematic, on-brand experience.
