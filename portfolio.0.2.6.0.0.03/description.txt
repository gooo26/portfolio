Commercial Staging Landing Page Template
A refined, minimal landing page template tailored for commercial office staging brands to showcase projects and capture leads.

Key features

Hero section with bold tagline, dual CTAs (“View Projects” and “Enquire Now”).
Approach section explaining philosophy, value props, and results-focused stats.
Portfolio grid highlighting staged offices by city and project type.
Services layout for furniture rental, concept & styling, and turnkey install.
Contact form with location, email, phone, and project details fields.
Primary use case

Ideal for staging studios or interior consultants who specialize in commercial real estate and need a polished, conversion-focused web presence to win new leases and projects.
